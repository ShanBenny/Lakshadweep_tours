Itinerary:

Day 1: Arrival at Agatti Island

Flight from Cochin to Agatti. Our representative will receive you and transfer you to the hotel. After a welcome drink, check-in and relax. After lunch, explore Agatti Island's attractions like the eastern side embarkation jetty, seawater thermal desalination plant, lagoon beach, and archaeological museum.

Day 2: Thinnakara & Bangaram Island Trip

After breakfast, take a boat transfer to Thinnakara Island (45 minutes). Spot dolphins and green turtles on the way. Enjoy sightseeing, shipwreck snorkeling, and lunch on board. Visit Bangaram Island for sightseeing and water sports (if available). Return to Agatti by 4 PM.

Day 3: Water Sports & Kalpitty Island Visit

Morning scuba diving adventure. After lunch, enjoy evening tea and head to Kalpitty Island (20-30 minutes by boat). Try fishing and explore the island.

Day 4: Departure

After breakfast, transfer to Agatti Airport for your return flight to Bangalore/Cochin.

Important Notes:

  • The islands are a restricted area, and a special Entry Permit is required to visit.
  • To obtain the permit, provide the following details: Names, Address, Date of Birth, Father's Name, Two Neighbors' Addresses, Aadhaar Copy, Passport Size Photos, and Police Clearance Certificate (Original).
  • Confirm the booking with 50% payment in advance.
  • Permit processing takes a minimum of 10 days, so send the required details at least 20 days before travel.
  • The entry permit is valid for 15 days from the date of issue.
